About Me

I'm Tina Graves, the artist behind Noi-Noi Paints. Ever since I could remember, I've always been fascinated by art, and the media I grew up loving has fueled my inspiration to want to share some of the interests I’ve always admired.

Media such as Anime, Video Games, Comic Books, and Kaiju, have been the backbone for my love to create. Therefore, I hope that through my paintings I can spread that same adoration and overall admiration of animation and media!
